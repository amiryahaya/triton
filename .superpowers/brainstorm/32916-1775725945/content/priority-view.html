A quarter's worth of work at a normal pace. More scrolling but fewer "I need to see more" clicks.

C

Everything, paginated server-side

Infinite scroll / "Load more" button fetching pages of 50 at a time. Most flexible, most complex to implement, and for realistic org sizes (≤10,000 findings) pagination is overkill.

Workflow features — explicitly out of scope for Phase 1

Things like "mark as in progress", "dismiss this finding", "assign to engineer" are deliberately deferred. They'd require a new finding_state table, user-scoped mutation flows, audit entries, and tests — enough work to be its own phase. Phase 1 stays read-only: see the priorities, act on them outside Triton, verify on the next scan.
