My recommendation · C (Inter + Geist)

All-sans, no serif anywhere. Geist handles display + numbers (tabular, no wobble in tables), Inter handles body. Modern without being generic. Ships with self-hosted .woff2 to stay CDN-free. If you want to collapse to a single family for simplicity, B (all-Inter) is the safest fallback.
